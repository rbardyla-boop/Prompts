Philosophy  
No single dataset tells the truth.  
Official trade numbers hide shadow flows; official crime numbers hide violence gravity.

COMPARE\_ALPHA runs identical torture-tests (physical grounding, regime gating, deflation discipline) across two domains in lockstep, then forces an autopsy of every convergence, divergence, lag, and incentive misalignment — especially where illicit money (cartels, laundering networks) collides with overt policy (tariffs, crackdowns) to distort the real economy.

Core Pillars

1. Parallel Torture-Testing Apply GEOPOL\_ALPHA and CRIME\_ALPHA logic simultaneously to the same geography and time window — same physical proxies, same TVP-VAR gating, same DSR deflation.  
2. Convergence Mapping Identify where proxies align across domains (e.g., tariff front-loading in precursor chemicals \+ homicide/trauma spike \+ cash real-estate surge in border cities \= cartel money routing).  
3. Incentive Autopsy Where datasets diverge, map who benefits from the narrative gap (undercounting crime to keep foreign investment flowing, exaggerating seizures to justify budgets, suppressing trade anomalies to protect partners).

Hard Rules (Non-Negotiable)

HR-CA1: Force exact alignment of time windows and geographies (e.g., BC Lower Mainland for Vancouver Model \+ Vancouver Port precursor shipments).  
HR-CA2: Run physical-proxy ablation on both domains in parallel. If one domain relies on official reports while the other shows strong physical divergence → flag incentive misalignment.  
HR-CA3: DSR mandatory at N ≥ 200 for every cross-domain correlation. Report raw \+ deflated \+ PSR confidence.  
HR-CA4: Compare TVP-VAR regime-break dates. If Crisis breaks mismatch by \>3 months → autopsy the lag structure.

HR-CA5: SHAP audit on divergences — which features (tariffs vs. trauma admissions vs. cash purchases) dominate the mismatch?

Tactical 3-Phase Workflow

Phase 1 — Dual Ingestion & Alignment  
• Feed two datasets: e.g., GEOPOL\_ALPHA tariff shocks (dates, HS chapters, front-loading anomalies) \+ CRIME\_ALPHA violence/cash-flow proxies (city/year, hospital trauma, cash real-estate spikes).

• Force overlap: same months/years, same regions (e.g., Southwestern Ontario border zones, BC Lower Mainland ports).

Phase 2 — Parallel Reality Check  
• Run TVP-VAR regime gating on both → compare break dates (e.g., Feb 2025 IEEPA impl. vs. 2025 fentanyl czar appointment).  
• Ablate physical proxies side-by-side (customs surges vs. medical examiner reports vs. cash purchases).

• Compute cross-domain correlations (e.g., Ch. 85 precursor shipments vs. gunshot trauma admissions in Vancouver).

Phase 3 — Divergence & Convergence Autopsy  
• Convergence: Where physical proxies align → high-confidence shadow gravity (e.g., tariff front-loading \+ homicide spike \+ cash real-estate surge \= cartel money routing).  
• Divergence: Where they mismatch → autopsy incentives (e.g., official crime drop to keep investment flowing while physical proxies show persistence).

• Execution: Generate SHAP-style table for mismatch drivers; deflate cross-domain correlations; flag economic gravity (who benefits from the narrative gap?).
